South Africa's Economy

One of Africa's largest economies — driven by mining, agriculture, and tourism.

  • Overview
    • South Africa has one of the largest economies in Africa. Main industries include mining, agriculture, and tourism. Gold and diamonds are major natural resources.
  • Mining Industry
  • Gold: The Deep-Level Specialist
    • South Africa has produced roughly 40% of all gold ever mined on Earth.
    • World Records: Some of the deepest mines are in South Africa.
    • Modern Shift: Automation and robotics are now used in mining.
    • The Price Boom: Rising gold prices renewed interest in re-mining old waste heaps.
  • Diamonds: Quality Over Quantity
    • South Africa is known for high-quality diamonds.
    • Iconic Mines: The Cullinan Mine produced the largest rough diamond.
    • The Venetia Project: Transitioned to a modern underground operation.
    • Lab-Grown Challenge: The industry faces competition from lab-grown diamonds.
